Welcome to Left 4 Dead 2 Fansite

Left 4 Dead 2 (abbreviated L4D2) is a single-player and multiplayer cooperative survival horror first person shooter game developed by Valve Corporation. It is the sequel to Left 4 Dead, and was built using Source engine. It was first announced on June 1, 2009 by Valve Corporation at Microsoft's E3 2009 press conference. The game is available on PC and Xbox 360.

Story

The story of Left 4 Dead 2 first takes place in Savannah, Georgia. After the past event in Left 4 Dead 1. The four survivor climbs to the rooftop to witness the last rescue helicopter leave them. This is where they greet and know each other. Namely: Ellis, Coach, Rochelle and Nick. After that they decided to find evacuations to go to another rescue place where they can find help. The first place is exiting the building and going to the mall where they find a sports car and ellis had an idea to fill it up with gas so they can make a drive to New Orleans where they heard to be the last standing city of America. The team found a bridge in Rayford where they met the first team survivors in Left 4 Dead 1. Namely: Francis, Louis and Zoey. Their one member Bill sacrificed his life to raise the bridge thats why hes no longer here. The current survivors needs to cross the bridge they must find another way to across and refill the generator. While battling the infected by the first team they began to refill the generator and after that the 2 team met again and help them lower the bridge.

The current team began to drive off the bridge to Griffin County, where they come ahead to the whispering oaks amusement park. Where they go to the peach pit that is abandoned by midnight riders. The survivors succeed to get the helicopters attention to help them while the infected swarm the stadium. After being rescued the pilot becomes infected so nick shoot him causing the helicopter to crash. After the crash they went to discover a small village where a lot of villagers where infected as they go ahead to the swamp they come across a lot of dead militaries and crashed planes. The previous survivors informed the passerby to go to the plantation house. Where they find a radio that helps them contact virgil for rescue. Before fending off the horde, virgil blasts the plantation gate open to allow the survivors to escape on his boat before the horde could get them. While on their way the boat began to low on gas so they stopped at ducatel, Mississippi to find gas. The gas station that has supply are around two miles away so they pass through an abandoned sugar mill where they found a lot of witches.

After collecting what they needed they began to go back but a heavy storm hits. Coach comes up with the idea to use the burger tanks signal to get virgils attention to pick them up before the swarm get them they left successfully. Virgil drops the team to New Orleans. After virgil dropped them they go outside of the safe house where they see jets and militaries in the area revealing that the military has not abandoned the city yet. They decided that the bridge is the best chance of survival but discovers that a lot of non-infected people corpses are on the way. They discovered that the military will bomb the bridge so they quickly pass across the bridge on all their power. As they pass the bridge they reached the 2 military the papa gator and rescue 7 who suspect that the survivors are carriers. Rescue 7 is prepared to carry the carriers, papa gator informs the survivor that they are lucky the military just left the are with them. As they are carried in the rescue helicopter the fighter jets bombed the bridge where they cross to prevent the infected from leaving the city and the pilot takes the survivors out to a military cruise ship at sea. This is where the story of Left 4 Dead 2 ends.

Coach is a forty-four year-old Physical Education teacher at the local high school in the town of Savannah―his home town. A prospective professional defensive linesman, a career-ending knee injury suffered at college led Coach to take up teaching as an alternative pathway into a possible pro-coaching football career. He wears a polo shirt in the yellow and purple colors of his high school employer, training gloves, beige slacks, and gray sneakers, as well as a gold chain necklace.

Ellis is a twenty-three-year-old mechanic hailing from Savannah, similar to Coach. He wears a Bull-Shifter T-shirt, a white and blue cap, with a truck towing logo (that is also seen in the car alarm section in The Parish), half-removed coveralls with the sleeves tied around his waist, and black work boots.

Nick was a thirty-five-year-old ex-prisoner who escaped in the wake of the Infection and celebrated his newfound freedom by stealing a white suit from a high-end retailer. He later developed into a shady con man and gambler drifting from city to city who bemoans his bad luck in happening to be passing through Savannah (and not Las Vegas) when the current crisis hit.

Rochelle is a twenty-nine-year-old minor associate producer for a news station with a high reputation, though her job had more to do with moving cables and coffee duties. She was in Savannah producing a segment about the evacuation center when the area became overrun with the Infected. She wears a pink T-shirt with a Depeche Mode logo, silver hoop earrings, blue jeans, a thin studded belt around her waist, two silver bracelets on her left arm and knee-high brown boots. She also keeps her hair tied in a bun.
